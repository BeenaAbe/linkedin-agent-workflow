# LinkedIn Content Automation - Technical Requirements

## Project Overview
Build an automated LinkedIn content creation and publishing system that generates high-performing posts based on proven content strategies, best practices, and optimal posting schedules.

---

## Core Functional Requirements

### 1. Content Type Engine
The system must support six distinct post types, each with specific workflows and outputs:

#### 1.1 Thought Leadership Posts
**Goal:** Establish authority, offer unique perspectives, drive high-quality conversation

**Content Requirements:**
- Single unique thesis/argument (e.g., "The future of marketing isn't AI tools; it's AI fluency")
- Evidence from proprietary data, industry reports, or authoritative quotes
- 4-5 point structure: Hook → Problem/Current Belief → Contrarian Thesis → 3 Supporting Points → CTA
- 1500+ character count
- Confident, educational tone

**Visual Format:**
- Carousel (5-15 slides) with unique frameworks, trend breakdowns, proprietary charts
- OR single branded quote card with contrarian take
- Aspect ratio: 1:1 (Square)

**Time Allocation:** 60+ minutes (High-effort)
- Phase I: Research & Thesis (40%) - 25 minutes
- Phase II: Creative Drafting (30%) - 18 minutes  
- Phase III: Polishing & CTA (30%) - 17 minutes

#### 1.2 Product Posts
**Goal:** Highlight product benefits, drive clicks/sign-ups

**Content Requirements:**
- Focus on ONE core value proposition (e.g., "Save 1 hour a week")
- Structure: Problem → Feature Name → Benefit (using bullet points)
- Social proof from early users when available
- Clear, specific CTA (e.g., "Start your free trial," "Watch the 2-min demo")
- Enthusiastic, benefit-focused tone

**Visual Format:**
- Native video (30-90 seconds) with subtitles showing "Aha!" moment
- OR screenshot/GIF of feature in high resolution
- Aspect ratio: 1:1 (Square) or 4:5 (Vertical)

**Time Allocation:** 50 minutes
- Phase I: Research & Asset Prep (20%) - 10 minutes
- Phase II: Creative Drafting (50%) - 25 minutes
- Phase III: Polishing & CTA (30%) - 15 minutes

#### 1.3 Personal Brand Posts
**Goal:** Build connection, humanize profile, share value-driven lessons

**Content Requirements:**
- Story structure: Beginning (Struggle/Challenge) → Middle (Turning Point) → End (Resolution/Insight)
- Start in media res (middle of action/emotion)
- Bridge personal anecdote to professional takeaway
- Emphasis on emotion and vulnerability
- Reflective CTA asking about reader's similar experience

**Visual Format:**
- Candid/authentic photo related to story
- High-quality professional yet approachable
- Aspect ratio: 1:1 (Square) or 4:5 (Vertical)

**Time Allocation:** 35 minutes
- Phase I: Research & Reflection (30%) - 10 minutes
- Phase II: Creative Drafting (50%) - 17 minutes
- Phase III: Polishing & CTA (20%) - 7 minutes

#### 1.4 Educational & How-To Posts
**Goal:** Provide actionable value, establish expertise

**Content Requirements:**
- Address common, small industry challenge solvable in 3-5 steps
- Start with result/promise (e.g., "Stop wasting time on X. Use this template...")
- Numbered lists or clear bullet points for maximum scannability
- Brief, direct descriptions with 'why' for each step
- CTA asking audience to try and report back

**Visual Format:**
- Carousel (3-7 slides) with step-by-step guides or checklists
- OR simple infographic summarizing tips
- Aspect ratio: 1:1 (Square)

**Time Allocation:** 25 minutes
- Phase I: Core Concept Selection (20%) - 5 minutes
- Phase II: Structured Drafting (50%) - 12 minutes
- Phase III: Review & Publish (30%) - 8 minutes

#### 1.5 Interactive & Engagement Posts
**Goal:** Drive high volumes of comments, reactions, shares for algorithm boost

**Content Requirements:**
- Polarizing, trending, or highly relatable topic
- Open-ended question or forced choice (avoid simple Yes/No)
- Short, punchy setup
- Explicit CTA: "Comment below" or "Explain your choice"
- Prepared thought-out comment to post immediately after

**Visual Format:**
- Text-only (works best for immediate commenting)
- OR contrarian quote card
- Aspect ratio: N/A or 1:1 (Square)

**Time Allocation:** 10 minutes
- Phase I: Relevance & Question Framing (40%) - 4 minutes
- Phase II: Creative Hook & Setup (40%) - 4 minutes
- Phase III: Finalizing & Activation (20%) - 2 minutes

#### 1.6 Inspirational & Relatability Posts
**Goal:** Foster trust, share core values, provide motivation

**Content Requirements:**
- Specific moment of struggle, breakthrough, or key advice
- Clear emotional arc: Problem → Turning Point → Resolution/Lesson
- Start with vulnerable/painful part
- Short, punchy sentences with lots of line breaks
- Link personal experience to professional lesson others can apply
- Reflective CTA about reader's journey
- Vulnerable but hopeful tone (not self-pitying)

**Visual Format:**
- Behind-the-scenes photo showing team culture, challenge, or reflection moment
- OR quote card highlighting profound lesson
- Aspect ratio: 1:1 (Square)

**Time Allocation:** 33 minutes
- Phase I: Reflection & Story Focus (30%) - 10 minutes
- Phase II: Narrative Drafting (50%) - 17 minutes
- Phase III: Tone & Connection (20%) - 6 minutes

## 2. Content Creation Workflow

### 2.1 Research Phase
**Required Inputs:**
- Goal definition: What should audience do after reading? (visit page, comment, connect, see as expert)
- Desired outcome: Engagement, Clicks, Awareness, or Trust
- Target audience: Who to reach? (pain points, aspirations, knowledge level)
- Core data: Facts, stats, quotes, or personal anecdotes for main argument

**Process:**
1. Define the goal
2. Identify the audience
3. Gather supporting evidence
4. Validate content relevance

### 2.2 Creative Phase
**Required Outputs:**
- Hook: First 1-2 lines to grab attention before "see more..."
  - Options: Intrigue, Controversy, Strong Claim, or Urgent Question
- Structure: Clear, scannable format
  - Short paragraphs, bullet points, numbered lists, strategic emojis
  - Common flow: Hook → Problem/Context → Solution/Insight → CTA
- Visuals: Professional assets that add value
  - Headshots, product screenshots, relevant graphs

### 2.3 Writing Phase
**Required Elements:**
- Body content matching goal tone (educational, enthusiastic, reflective)
- Simple, direct voice
- Clear CTA aligned with goal
  - Examples: "Hit 'Like' if you agree," "Comment below with your biggest challenge," "Link in comments"
- 3-5 relevant, high-impact hashtags
  - Mix of broad, specific, and niche tags
- Final proofread for clarity, grammar, line breaks

## 3. Algorithm Optimization Requirements

### 3.1 Core Metrics to Optimize
**Primary Metrics:**
- **Dwell Time:** Duration users spend reading before scrolling
  - Optimize with: Carousel posts (PDFs), Native video
- **Initial Engagement:** Reactions, shares, comments within first 60-90 minutes
  - Optimize with: Strong CTA, optimal timing, comment encouragement

### 3.2 Native Content Rules (CRITICAL)
**MUST FOLLOW:**
- Upload videos directly to LinkedIn (never YouTube/Vimeo links)
- Place external links ONLY in first comment, state "Link in comments below" in post
- Use LinkedIn Polls and Carousel Posts (multi-page PDF)
- NO external URLs in main post text

### 3.3 Formatting for Scannability
**Hook Requirements:**
- First 1-2 lines (before "See more...") must be compelling
- Use strong statement, provocative question, or high-value number

**Structure Requirements:**
- Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences maximum)
- NO walls of text
- Bullet points and numbered lists
- Strategic emoji use
- Abundant line breaks

### 3.4 Comment Engagement Strategy
**Immediate Actions (First Hour):**
- Respond promptly to all comments
- Ask follow-up questions instead of simple "Thanks"
- Add related insights to keep conversation moving
- Plan for 30 minutes of active engagement management

## 4. Posting Schedule Requirements

### 4.1 Optimal Time Windows
System must support scheduling for these peak engagement periods:

| Time Window | Reason | Best Content Types |
|------------|--------|-------------------|
| 7:30-9:00 AM (Local) | Pre-work check-in, high traffic | Thought Leadership, Educational |
| 11:30 AM-1:00 PM (Local) | Lunch break scrolling | Interactive/Polls, Product Announcements |
| 4:30-6:00 PM (Local) | End of workday, commute | Personal Brand, Inspirational |

### 4.2 Day-of-Week Strategy
**Best Days (Highest Engagement):**
- Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- Use for Thought Leadership and major announcements

**Decent Days:**
- Monday: Industry news roundups
- Friday: Light, reflective, inspirational posts

**Avoid:**
- Weekends (lower professional network engagement)

### 4.3 Content Mix Schedule
**Weekly Rotation Example:**
- **Tuesday Morning:** Thought Leadership (Carousel) - Maximize Dwell Time
- **Wednesday Midday:** Interactive/Engagement (Poll/Question) - Maximize Comments
- **Thursday Morning/Afternoon:** Product/How-To (Native Video) - Maximize Clicks/Saves

**Consistency Rule:** Maintain regular schedule (3-5 posts/week) for algorithmic trust

## 5. Visual Asset Requirements

### 5.1 Technical Specifications
**Preferred Aspect Ratios:**
- Square (1:1): Default for carousels, images, quote cards
- Vertical (4:5 or 9:16): Better mobile screen real estate
- Avoid Landscape (1.91:1): Standard but less scroll-stopping

**Quality Standards:**
- Large, high-contrast text
- Clean brand aesthetic
- Professional yet approachable
- High resolution for all images
- Subtitles required for all videos

### 5.2 Format-Specific Requirements

**Native Video:**
- Duration: 30-90 seconds
- Must include subtitles
- Show "Aha!" moment clearly
- Upload directly to LinkedIn

**Carousel Posts:**
- Upload as multi-page PDF document
- 3-15 slides depending on content type
- Consistent design across slides
- Clear progression/storytelling

**Screenshots/GIFs:**
- High resolution
- Clear product UI visibility
- Annotated if needed for clarity

## 9. Error Prevention & Validation

### Content Validation Rules
**MUST Prevent:**
- External links in main post body
- Walls of text (> 3 sentences without breaks)
- Weak hooks (generic statements)
- Missing CTAs
- 0-2 hashtags (too few) or 6+ hashtags (too many)
- Non-native video links
- Missing engagement plan

### Quality Assurance Checks
**Before Publishing:**
- Read content aloud for clarity
- Verify tone matches content type
- Confirm CTA is specific and actionable
- Check all formatting (line breaks, emojis, bullets)
- Validate visual asset quality
- Ensure native content compliance

## 10. Analytics & Optimization

### Tracking Requirements
**Must Track Per Post:**
- Reach (impressions)
- Engagement rate (reactions + comments + shares / impressions)
- Dwell time (when available)
- Click-through rate (for product/link posts)
- Conversion rate (for specific CTAs)
- Comment sentiment
- Share velocity

### Optimization Feedback Loop
**System Must:**
- Analyze performance by content type
- Identify best-performing hooks
- Track optimal posting times for specific audience
- Measure engagement by day of week
- Compare native vs. linked content performance
- Adjust recommendations based on historical data
